Founding My Own Arabic Club!

Being trilingual is one of the things that I am most proud of, I wanted to share my knowledge with others. I thought that an Arabic Club would help students become more open to other cultures and ideas, allowing for diversity within our school. So I founded the Arabic Club at Shortridge High School and taught Arabic to many students after school on Thursdays.
